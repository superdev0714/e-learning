There are 4 solutions on the platform:

  • E-learnings

  • Exams

  • Presentations

  • Download Materials

E-learnings

To access the e-learnings select „E-Learnings”. It is on the right side of the User’s Panel.

Whenever you click continue e-learning, you start a new session or continue the unfinished session.

Each session of the e-learning has the following 4 phases:

  1. Repetitions: where you answer the questions from previous sessions prepared by our algorithm
  2. New Slides: where you see the new learning materials
  3. New Questions: where you see the questions to these materials
  4. Corrections: where you answer the questions answered wrongly on current session till success

When using on mobile always use it horinzontally

Always use view full screen for the best visibility of the slides

When using on PC, use arrows on keyboard to navigate between slides. On mobile or tablet use your finger

Use it everyday! It is the most efficient for your memory and you will avoid big accumulations of daily repetitions

Exams

To access the e-learnings select „Exams”. It is on the right side of the User’s Panel.

To start an exam click start.

This view will appear. Please give a nickname, how you will identify the job candidate and click start exam.

Now give the computer to the job candidate and come back when the exam is finished.

Your administrator sets the time and number of questions in the exam.

When the exam is finished (either all the questions were answered or all the time elapsed), The candidate will be able to see his result.

If you want to compare the result of this candidate to other candidates simply click on „GO TO EXAM SCORE LIST”.

To avoid accidental checking of the results by the candidates. You need to enter the password again to access that view.

In this view you can compare the results of the candidates.

You can also access the scores of your candidates directly from the User’s Panel by clicking the „SHOW EXAM SCORES” button.
